FAQ

Common questions

Does my video get uploaded anywhere?

No. Your video never leaves your machine. The only data sent externally is the transcript text to OpenAI for analysis — and that's done with store=false so OpenAI doesn't retain it either.

What does the OpenAI API cost?

Typically $0.20–$0.50 per hour of content using the SAFE profile. You control this entirely — it comes directly out of your own OpenAI account.

Do I need DaVinci Resolve?

No. Resolve integration is optional. You can export 9:16 MP4 clips with burned-in captions without Resolve. The Resolve integration is a bonus for professional editors.

What GPU do I need?

An NVIDIA RTX GPU with 8GB+ VRAM is recommended. RTX 3080 and above works great. The app will fall back to CPU processing if no NVIDIA GPU is found, but it will be slower.

Is this a subscription?

No. You pay once and own the software. The Professional tier includes lifetime updates. The Creator tier includes 12 months of updates.

Can I use it on multiple computers?

Each license is valid for one PC. Contact support to transfer your license to a new machine.
